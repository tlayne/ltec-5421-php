Bio

This is my 2nd semester as a Learning Technologies Master's Student. I began the program in the Fall of 2017, and hope to graduate in December of 2018. Currently I'm employed as the Assistant Director, Technology Support for Lamar University's Distance Learning Dept. In this role, I help maintain our LMS while researching new hardware and software solutions to better assist faculty and students in teaching and learning with technology. Previously, I worked as an instructional designer, and before that I was a web content manager for an online retailer. Outside of work, I'm the father of a beautiful baby girl who amazes me constantly. When I get the rare moment of free time, I enjoy listening to podcasts, gaming, and writing.
